## 1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Shoshin ("**we**", "**us**", "**our**", or "**Company**"). Shoshin is a mobile application designed to help users build and maintain morning routines through daily checkpoints, reflections, streak tracking, group communities, and social sharing.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains:
- What information we collect
- How we use your information
- How we protect your information
- Your rights and choices regarding your data

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the end of this document.

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

### **2.1 Account Information**

When you create a Shoshin account, we collect:

```
Required Information:
├─ Phone number (for OTP authentication)
└─ OR Email address (for Google OAuth login)

Optional Information:
├─ Full name
├─ Profile picture/avatar
├─ Location (optional)
└─ Bio/About section
```

**Why we collect this:**
- To authenticate and verify your identity
- To provide account access and recovery
- To enable social features (groups, sharing)
- To provide better user experience

### **2.2 Content You Create**

Shoshin allows you to create personal content:

```
Content Types:
├─ Checkpoint Photos
│  └─ Daily photos you take as part of your routine
│
├─ Reflections
│  └─ Written notes and journal entries about your morning
│
├─ Group Posts
│  └─ Content shared within group communities
│
└─ Captions & Text
   └─ Any text you add to your posts
```

**Important:** You retain full ownership of all content you create. We store this content to:
- Display it in your app
- Enable offline-first functionality
- Sync across your devices
- Support group sharing
- Back up your data

### **2.3 Photos & Media**

```
What we collect:
├─ Photos you capture via the app camera
├─ Photo metadata (timestamp, device info)
├─ Image data when compressed for storage
└─ Social media share data

Storage:
├─ Photos stored locally on your device FIRST
├─ Then synced to Firebase Cloud Storage
├─ Organized by: /checkpoints/{userId}/{photoId}/
└─ You can delete anytime
```

### **2.4 Habit & Streak Data**

We track and store:

```
Tracked Data:
├─ Streak count (consecutive days)
├─ Best streak record (all-time)
├─ Daily checkpoints (count)
├─ Consistency calendar/heatmap
├─ Timestamps of activities
```

**Why:** To show your progress, calculate streaks, create heatmaps, and enable group leaderboards.

### **2.5 Group & Community Data**

When using Groups feature:

```
Group Data Collected:
```

When you share content:

## 4. HOW WE STORE YOUR DATA

### **4.1 Local Storage (On Your Device)**

```
Data stored locally:
├─ Your account information (cached)
├─ All photos (original + compressed)
├─ All reflections/notes
├─ Streak data
├─ Group memberships
├─ Sync queue (pending uploads)
└─ App preferences

Storage location:
├─ Android App Data directory
├─ Protected by device encryption
├─ Only accessible by Shoshin app
└─ You can clear anytime (Settings)

Benefits:
✅ Works offline
✅ Fast access
✅ Private on your device
✅ You control deletion
```

### **4.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase)**

```
Cloud Services Used:
├─ Firebase Firestore (database)
├─ Firebase Cloud Storage (photos)
├─ Firebase Authentication
└─ Firebase Cloud Messaging

Where data is stored:
├─ US data center (default)
├─ Encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
├─ Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
└─ Geographically redundant

Organization:
├─ /users/{userId}/
├─ /groups/{groupId}/
├─ /checkpoints/{userId}/
├─ /reflections/{userId}/
└─ /social_shares/{userId}/
```

### **4.3 Data Retention**

```
How long we keep data:

Active Accounts:
├─ Keep all data while account is active
└─ Data remains as long as you use the app

Inactive Accounts (12 months+):
├─ We may delete data
├─ We'll notify you before deletion
└─ You can request export before deletion

User Deletion Request:
├─ Delete all personal data within 30 days
├─ Retain only aggregate anonymized data
├─ Cannot be reversed
└─ You'll receive confirmation

Photos:
├─ Stored as long as you keep account
├─ Can delete individually anytime
├─ Bulk delete via Settings
└─ Deleted copies cannot be recovered
```

## DATA DELETION

### How to Delete Your Account and Data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

**In-App Deletion:**
1. Open Shoshin app
2. Go to Profile → Settings
3. Scroll to "Privacy & Data"
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm deletion
6. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Email Request:**
If you cannot access the app, email us at cobwebtechnologies1@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered phone number or email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

**What Gets Deleted:**
- Your profile and personal information
- All checkpoints and streak history
- All photos and reflections
- Group memberships
- Friend connections
- Analytics data

**What Cannot Be Deleted:**
- Aggregated anonymous analytics (no personal identifiers)
- Legal compliance records (kept for 7 years as required by law)
